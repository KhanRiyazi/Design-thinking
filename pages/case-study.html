Define & Conceptualize

Transforming research insights into clear problem statements and structured solution concepts.

Phase Overview

With research insights in hand, this stage focuses on defining the core problem and conceptualizing solutions. It’s where user personas, journey maps, and problem statements come to life. Clarity at this stage ensures we're solving the right problems in the right ways.

Conceptualization Methods

  • • User Personas & Empathy Mapping
  • • Journey Mapping & Service Blueprints
  • • "How Might We" Questions
  • • Problem Statements & Insight Clustering
  • • Brainstorming & Early Concept Sketches

Define & Conceptualize Framework

Goals
  • Align the team on a clear problem definition
  • Prioritize user pain points & opportunities
  • Create a foundation for ideation
Techniques
  • Persona development from research data
  • Customer journey & touchpoint mapping
  • Reframing problems into opportunities
  • Insight-driven brainstorming prompts
Outputs
  • Personas & journey maps
  • Problem statements
  • Opportunity areas for ideation
  • Initial concept sketches
