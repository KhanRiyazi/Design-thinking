About This Stage

This is where creativity meets strategy. I generate a wide range of ideas through brainstorming sessions, then refine them into tangible designs through wireframes, mockups, and prototypes.

Key Activities

  • Brainstorming multiple solution ideas
  • Sketching initial concepts
  • Building wireframes to visualize layouts
  • Creating mockups for polished designs
  • Developing interactive prototypes for testing

Tools I Use

  • Figma – for collaborative design & prototyping
  • Adobe Creative Suite – for high-quality visuals
  • Pen & Paper – quick idea sketching
